# CoreBar Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

CoreBar does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

CoreBar runs locally on your Mac and displays system usage information such as CPU, memory, and disk usage. This information is processed on device and is not uploaded to any server.

CoreBar does not require an account, does not include analytics, does not track users, and does not use third-party advertising services.

If you have questions, contact the developer through the support page:

https://github.com/MightyKartz/CoreBar/issues
